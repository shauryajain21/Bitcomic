How to make your own coin (scam alerrt!!)

Making crypto was hard, in 2009. Thanks to ethereum, We can make as many cryptocurrency as we can, in 10 mins. We can make a token (ERC-20) that runs on ethereum blockchain. So on the ethereum blockchain, we can have as many coins running as we want. One is ethereum itself, the mighty.

But how...?

Through some programs written in solidity (ethereum’s programming language), we can create our own coin by setting in the parameters such as name, symbol, and the total amount of coins that will exist. This is very very easy. If you are a tech guy, you can create a coin in minutes by copying publicly available code. If you aren’t you can create on website like token jenny in less than 15 seconds.

What does this mean?

There can be SCAMS. A lot of scams. People can create a coin, hold 50% of it’s supply and list the rest 50% on exchanges. People can buy this coin under the assumption that it will go up in price because famous people are buying it and crypto gurus are saying it’s going to the moon. As soon as the coin reaches a substantial amount, The creator will cash out and sell their 50%, hence the coin crashing and people losing all their money. These are scam coins. Even if the creator does not intend to sell the coin or do some sort of scam, these coins still don’t have any use case until they are supporting something tangible like a startup or some new technology so it is real smart and big investors invest in them and ultimately it going up.

This is done almost all the time. People supporting random coins and spreading rumors about it going to the moon is devastating for the crypto economy. Let’s take a step back and check if these coins are ERC-20 tokens or BEP-20(bincance chain) or TRX-20(tron chain) tokens. If they are, see their use case.. What are they doing?Are they faking charity initiatives to hook you in? See all the factors before investing.

A rule of thumb is not to invest in any coin beyond top 50(shiba inu and many other shitcoins are there in the list so yeah check about them also) in your first crypto year. Invest safely.
