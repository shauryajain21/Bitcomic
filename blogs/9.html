NFTs

Non Fungible Tokens

Non-fungible more or less means Unique. Non fungible tokens mean Unique tokens. This uniqueness is achieved by *Drumroll* blockchain.

First, let’s start on the topic of what gives something digital value when it can be so easily replicated (eg- screenshots).

So where does mona lisa get's it value? The louvre museum it is stored.

So where does digital art get's its value? The blockchian technology where it is stored,

but how..?

NFTs can really be anything digital (such as drawings, music, your brain downloaded and turned into an AI)
for example, this Charizard pokemon card can be converted into an NFT.

NFTs are stored on a blockchain.

So this particular card is added to the blockchain, with its unique 32 digit key as well as its creator's 32 digit key.

It is now on the blockchain and anyone can buy it for the amount user specified.

when a user buys this NFT with his key, that key is attached to the card thus the sale is locked and becomes proof of ownership.

Users can now resale this NFT freely to anyone else and also the original creator gets a percentage(which the creator specifies) of the sale.

This is how NFTs are becoming the next big thing, reducing the diffrence between reality and virtuality.
